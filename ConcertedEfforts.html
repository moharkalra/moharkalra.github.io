Concerted Efforts

Concerted Efforts, which I am currently in the process of producing, is the lightest of the comics I’ve made. Based on my own experiences attending my first concert (which happened to be a Lil’ Dicky concert at Georgia Tech’s Homecoming 2017), I am trying to make a poppy, stylistically energetic comic centering on the various overwhelming and amusing sensory stimuli one

experiences at a concert. Inspired to an extent by Scott Pilgrim Vs. the World, I’m hoping to put on the page the energy and fun of a concert from the somewhat detached perspective of a non-fan. This comic will ultimately be 32 pages long and below are the first 10 pages.
