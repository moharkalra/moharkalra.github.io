I'm an artist and researcher exploring how we are poetically, humorously, and ethically entangled with the technological and ecological systems that scaffold our lives. I currently make interactive installations that try to foster a more personal, vernacular knowledge of the natural and unnatural spaces that surround us every day.

I'm currently based in Bremen, Germany.
