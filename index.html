I'm an artist and researcher exploring how we are poetically, humorously, or ethically entangled with the technological, biological, ecological and digital systems that scaffold our lives. I currently make interactive installations that try to foster a more personal, vernacular knowledge of the natural and unnatural spaces that surround us.

I have previously exhibited at Maajaam, the Narva Art Residency, and Medienfrische. I'm currently based in Bremen, Germany, pursuing a Master's in Digital Media at the HfK Bremen
