TechnoBabble

Technobabble is the comic strip I started at Georgia Tech’s student newspaper, The Technique. The comic has been a valuable outlet for me to produce short, humorous comics exploring happenstance occurrences and absurd observances I’ve had as

a new student at Georgia Tech. As these strips are easier to produce than my long form works, Technobabble has also helped me experiment with a quicker, more cartoony style. Below are all the published strips of Technobabble.
