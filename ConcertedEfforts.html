Concerted Efforts

Concerted Efforts, which is finished as of May 2018, is the lightest of the comics I’ve made. Based on my own experiences attending my first concert (which happened to be a Lil’ Dicky concert at Georgia Tech’s Homecoming 2017), I am trying to make a poppy, stylistically energetic comic centering on the various overwhelming and amusing sensory stimuli one

experiences at a concert. Inspired to an extent by Scott Pilgrim Vs. the World, I’m hoping to put on the page the energy and fun of a concert from the somewhat detached perspective of a non-fan.
