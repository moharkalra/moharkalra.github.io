Boxes

Boxes was a 24-page comic finished in April 2019, inspired by the melancholy I felt moving out of the AirBNB in St. Pete, Florida that I was staying in during Summer 2018. This comic also represents my first experimentation with fold-outs, inserts and the physical affordances of the comic medium. All pages are photographed below.
